My real life
2026-04-21 | life in real

To be honest, I’m not doing very well in real life.
I live in a residential area in the suburbs of Shanghai.
From a young age, my grades were never very good, and the same thing happened in junior high school.
From the days when friends were inseparable to the estrangement of today, I have lived through—and lost—far too much…
Shanghai is small, so I can go anywhere to temporarily escape my original self.
I am deeply immersed in the world of anime and manga, and I occasionally participate in pop-up events.
However, since I am not yet an adult, attending full-scale anime conventions remains somewhat difficult for me.
After June 2026, I intend to transition entirely to an online presence; aside from participating in events, I have no desire to do anything else.
My life goes on, I just hope things get a little better from here on out.

我经历了太多，也失去了太多…
上海很小，所以我可以去上海的任何地方来暂时逃离我原本的自我

苦命还在继续，但愿接下来的日子能过的好一点罢
